Protein Pancakes

A delicuis healthy breakfast with good macro nutrients

  • one cup of pancake mix
  • One cup of milk
  • a scoop of weigh protein poweder
  • butter
  • pan
  1. mix pancake mix and protein powder together
  2. mix in your cup of milk
  3. cover pan with butter
  4. add mixture onto pan
  5. flip when bubbles start forming
  6. serve when both sides are a golden brown :3
